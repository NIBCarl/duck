Usage Guidelines

DO:

  • Use the official SKI MASK DUCK logo in its entirety
  • Maintain the aspect ratio when resizing
  • Leave appropriate padding around the logo
  • Use the provided color palette for branded materials
  • Use the logo on appropriate backgrounds that maintain visibility

DON'T:

  • Modify or alter the logo in any way
  • Stretch or distort the proportions
  • Change the colors of the logo
  • Add effects like shadows or glows
  • Use the logo on busy backgrounds that reduce visibility
  • Use the logo in a way that implies partnership without permission
