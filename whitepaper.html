1. Introduction

SKI MASK DUCK ($SKI) is a community-driven cryptocurrency token built on the Ethereum blockchain. Launched in 2023, SKI MASK DUCK represents the fusion of meme culture with genuine utility and community governance in the decentralized finance (DeFi) space.

The character of the SKI MASK DUCK—a duck wearing a ski mask—symbolizes the core values of our project: privacy, financial freedom, and the irreverent spirit that makes cryptocurrency culture unique. While the project began as a meme coin, our ambitions extend far beyond, with plans for staking mechanisms, DAO governance, and a vibrant ecosystem of products and services.

This whitepaper outlines the fundamental aspects of the SKI MASK DUCK project, providing transparency about our tokenomics, technology, governance structure, and future roadmap.

SKI MASK DUCK is committed to true decentralization through community governance.

Current Governance

During the initial phases, governance decisions are made by the founding team with extensive community input through:
